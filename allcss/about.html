NEW J.A.PRIVATE ITI Kaushambi is proposed with the sole objective of providing skills in the various vocational trades to fulfill the skilled manpower requirement of Industry. There are 4 trades (ELECTRICIAN,ELECTRICIAN POWER DISTRIBUTION,IOT TECHNICIAN(SMART HEALTH CARE) & FASHION DESIGN AND TECHNOLOGY and the aim is to go upto 10 trades in next 5 years. The courses are affiliated to National Council for Vocational Training (N.C.V.T) through the Directorate General of Employment and Training (D.G.E.T), Government of India in the Ministry of labour, New Delhi. The institute has all infrastructure facilities needed for overall development of skilled workers, like library, audio visual aids, and hi-tech computer lab for awareness about computers to trainees of all trades.
